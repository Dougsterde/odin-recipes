Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe



Awesome grilled cheese sandwich recipe.

List of ingredients.

  • bread (2 slices)
  • butter
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Butter the both slices of bread
  2. Place one slice of buttered bread in a hot pan
  3. Place slice of cheese on butter
  4. Place second slice of buttered bread on top
  5. When the side facing down is browned and cheese is melted flip and brown other side
  6. When done remove from pan and serve
